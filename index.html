Welcome to Scenery

Welcome to Scenery Finder! This site was created to help you find to view-tiful places. Scenery Finder was made by three students in a web development bootcamp. The goal was to make a site that could help others. The creators wanted to make a site that fit their interest in the hope of helping someone else out. This site is filled with photos and links to help you find your next adventure. Hope you enjoy it!
